# Privacy Policy

**Last updated: April 2, 2026**

LinguaSync ("the App") is developed by Eduardo Rodas. This Privacy Policy explains how the App collects, uses, and protects your information.

## Information We Collect

### Speech Data
The App uses your device's microphone to capture speech for real-time translation. Audio is processed on-device using Apple's Speech Recognition framework. Transcribed text is sent to a third-party translation API (Anthropic) to generate translations. Audio recordings are not stored or transmitted — only the transcribed text is sent for translation.

### Translation Data
Transcribed text is sent to Anthropic's API solely for the purpose of translation. We do not store, log, or retain any translated text on our servers. Anthropic's data handling is governed by their own privacy policy (https://www.anthropic.com/privacy).

### Subscription Data
Subscription purchases and management are handled through Apple's App Store and RevenueCat. We do not directly collect or store payment information. RevenueCat may collect anonymous usage identifiers to manage subscription status. See RevenueCat's privacy policy at https://www.revenuecat.com/privacy.

### Usage Data
The App tracks a daily translation count stored locally on your device using standard on-device storage. This data never leaves your device.

## Information We Do Not Collect

- We do not collect personal information such as your name, email, or phone number.
- We do not collect location data.
- We do not use analytics or advertising SDKs.
- We do not sell, share, or rent any user data to third parties.

## Data Storage

All app data (speaker names, language preferences, usage counts) is stored locally on your device. No user data is stored on external servers controlled by us.

## Third-Party Services

The App uses the following third-party services:

- **Apple Speech Recognition** — for on-device speech-to-text processing
- **Anthropic API** — for text translation (transcribed text is sent to their servers)
- **RevenueCat** — for subscription management
- **Apple App Store** — for payment processing

Each third-party service is governed by its own privacy policy.

## Children's Privacy

The App is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children.

## Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above.
