## Common Questions

**How does LinguaSync work?**
Set each speaker's language, tap the mic, and speak. Your words are translated and spoken aloud in the other person's language in real time.

**What languages are supported?**
English, Spanish, French, German, Italian, Portuguese, Chinese (Simplified & Traditional), Japanese, Korean, Arabic, Hindi, and more.

**How do I manage my subscription?**
Open the App, tap the gear icon on the setup screen, or go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your device.

**How do I cancel my subscription?**
Go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions > LinguaSync Pro > Cancel Subscription.
